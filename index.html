The server is now running a plugin that removes the player's projectiles when they die. On many occasions, the "losing" player is given an unfair advantage in the next fight because of their post-death rockets, but no longer is this the case. While ultimately a minor feature, I'm interested in hearing any opinions people might have. You can visit AlliedMods for more information on the plugin itself:
https://forums.alliedmods.net/showthread.php?t=344992




23rd DEC 2023

As this is the first news post, here is a great opportunity to talk about some of the inspirations behind MGE.ME the server!

TF2 Classic has a small but dedicated community of players who want to play TF2 with improved performance, better developer support, fewer cheaters, and the new maps and weapons that hard-working modders have made. With the excitement around TF2C, some people might be open to trying MGE in a more casual environment, while others might have never heard of it before. MGE.ME is here to offer all TF2C fans an action-packed gamemode with the ultimate goal of kindling a deeper appreciation for the mechanical depth of Team Fortress 2.
